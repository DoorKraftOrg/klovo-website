When a general contractor installs garage cabinets in a custom home, they're making a bet with their reputation. The cabinet system has to survive the final walkthrough, the first year of homeowner use, and the inevitable "I called the builder" phone call when something goes wrong. That's why a growing number of contractors and custom home builders are choosing KLOVO's modular garage cabinet system — and the reasons have less to do with aesthetics and everything to do with job site realities.

## The Contractor's Cabinet Problem

Builders face a unique set of constraints that homeowners never think about. Time on the job site is money. Every hour a crew spends assembling garage cabinets is an hour not spent on trim, painting, or punch list items. Traditional garage cabinets create three specific problems for contractors:

1. **Assembly labor costs.** Most garage cabinet systems require 4-8 hours of skilled labor to assemble and install a full garage setup. At $75-150/hour for a finish carpenter, that's $300-$1,200 in labor before the first door is hung.
2. **Warranty callbacks.** Steel cabinets rust at the base within 18-24 months in humid climates. Particle board cabinets swell when garage moisture hits them. Each callback costs the builder $200-500 in crew time, materials, and customer goodwill.
3. **Client customization demands.** Every homeowner wants a different garage layout. Fixed-size cabinet systems force the builder to either say no to customization or order custom millwork at 3-5x the cost.

## Why KLOVO Solves the Builder Equation

### Zero Warranty Callbacks on Cabinet Failures

KLOVO cabinets use thermally fused laminate (TFL) with PVC edge-banding — the same material system used in commercial kitchen cabinetry. TFL doesn't rust like steel, doesn't swell like particle board, and doesn't crack like melamine when exposed to garage temperature extremes. Each cabinet holds up to 500 lbs, which means homeowners can load them with power tools, paint cans, and bulk storage without sag or failure.

For builders, this translates to a simple value proposition: install KLOVO cabinets, and garage cabinets disappear from your warranty callback list. The soft-close hinges and drawer slides are rated for 50,000+ cycles, so even the homeowner who opens their tool cabinet ten times a day won't wear out the hardware in the first decade.

### Modular Configuration for Every Garage Layout

Every custom home has a different garage footprint. A 2-car garage in a starter home needs a different cabinet layout than a 3-car garage in a luxury build. KLOVO's modular system lets builders configure exact layouts from a standard set of components — base cabinets, wall cabinets, tall cabinets, and accessories — without ordering custom millwork.

This modularity means a builder can offer "included garage storage" as a standard feature in every home, customize the layout per lot, and keep the SKU count manageable for purchasing and inventory. It's the difference between offering a feature and managing a nightmare.
